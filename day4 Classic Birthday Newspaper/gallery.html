Milestone Birthdays of Thalapathy

The Birthday Times

June 22, 2004

Rise as a Mass Star

By this year, Vijay had delivered several blockbuster hits like "Ghilli" (2004) and "Thirupaachi" (2005), cementing his place as a leading mass hero in Tamil cinema.

The Birthday Times

June 22, 2017

The Mersal Phenomenon

Vijay’s birthday in 2017 was extra special for fans, as his much-anticipated movie "Mersal" was set to release. The film went on to become a massive hit, earning over ₹250 crores globally and creating a record for Vijay's career.

The Birthday Times

June 22, 2019

Master Success and Social Impact

Vijay’s 47th birthday was celebrated with the backdrop of his film "Master" becoming a post-pandemic blockbuster. Despite COVID-19 restrictions, fans celebrated virtually with digital fan events and social media trends.
